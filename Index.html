"Passing on the savings to you!"

In 1968 with a few cars, a little business knowlege, and a love of automobiles, Patrick Murphy started Murphy's Auto and opened his first lot on Fremont Street in Las Vegas, Nevada. Murphy's Auto has since grown to 20 dealerships across the state of Nevada. Now run by Patrick's grandsons, Patrick and Kevin, or The Boys as he calls them, Murphy's Auto is a world class auto dealership where quality and savings are the principles we live by.
